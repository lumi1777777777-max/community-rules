Official Department Policy

Community Team Rules

All Community Division members must follow these rules to maintain professionalism, fairness, and high-quality roleplay across the city.

The Community Team represents the public image of the server. Members are expected to act maturely, communicate clearly, and create positive experiences for all players.

01

Professional Conduct

Members must remain respectful, mature, and calm at all times. Toxicity, harassment, discrimination, drama, or disrespect will not be tolerated.

02

Activity Requirements

Members are expected to stay active in the city and assist with division activities regularly.

  • Attend events whenever possible
  • Notify leadership about inactivity
  • Support other team members
03

Event Approval

Large events must be approved by Community Leadership before being publicly announced or hosted.

04

Fairness & No Favoritism

All players must be treated equally. Members may not abuse their position, unfairly reward friends, or manipulate event results.

05

Communication Standards

All announcements, Discord messages, and voice communication must remain clear, respectful, and professional.

06

Roleplay Quality

Community Team members must encourage immersive RP and follow all server rules including no RDM, no VDM, no exploiting, no metagaming, and no powergaming.

07

Event Control

Hosts must keep events organized and controlled. If players disrupt the event, warn them professionally and contact staff when needed.

08

Confidentiality

Internal staff discussions, private event plans, server information, and leadership decisions must not be leaked.

09

Teamwork

Members must work together respectfully, support event hosts, and follow leadership instructions during events.

10

Community First

The goal of the division is to create enjoyable, memorable, and welcoming experiences for the entire city.

Disciplinary Action

Failure to follow these rules may result in a verbal warning, written warning, suspension, demotion, or removal from the Community Division. Severe misconduct may lead to immediate removal.

Community Division — Stay Professional. Stay Active. Build the Community.
